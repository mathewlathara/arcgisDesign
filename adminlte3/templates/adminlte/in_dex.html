What is DeX-Terity

A machine-learning, data-intensive analytical tool, DeX-Terity provides hindsight, insight and foresight into the complexities of watershed health. Our platform analyzes multidisciplinary data to explain and visualize the impacts of a variety of anthropogenic stressors, predicting trends over time and prescribing actions today to protect and improve water quality and watershed health into the future.
