Welcome to DeX-Terity

What is DeX-Terity?

A machine-learning, data-intensive analytical tool, DeX-Terity provides hindsight, insight and foresight into the complexities of watershed health. Our platform analyzes multidisciplinary data to explain and visualize the impacts of a variety of anthropogenic stressors, predicting trends over time and prescribing actions today to protect and improve water quality and watershed health into the future

Why use DeX-Terity?

DeX-Terity can help you compare andvisualize a wide range of environmental andsocloeconomic data, and understandwatershed trends and Impacts in real-time!The platform provides the hindsight, insightand foresight critical for predicting potentialrisks to water quallty and watershed healthbefore they happen and prescribingsustainable actions.

How does DeX-Terity work?

DeXTerity's machine learning models find patterns In historical data, connecting a wide range of varlables with specific targets (e.g.,water quality metrics) to understand potential Impacts at different spatial resolutions (e.g., conservation authority boundarles, watershed, etc.). The platform's models are also trained to adjust the weight of each varlable, In order to make predictions about new or unseen data.
